Credit facility represented by the `credit_token`, providing controlled access to issuer account liquidity. Capitalised terms used in this summary and not otherwise defined have the meanings given in the Common Definitions Schedule.

- **Lender**: Issuer group grants a revolving credit line to holders of the NFT.
- **Facility limit**: Determined by swap arrangements; initial deployment aligns with AUD 90 capital advance tied to the factored invoice.
- **Drawdown rights**: Exercised via platform operations that authorise transfers from the issuer account, subject to collateral coverage ratios and swap obligations.
- **Security**: Supported by collateral assignments and the transfer of the invoice receivable into the collateral group.
- **Covenants**: NFT holders must maintain group membership compliance and observe usage restrictions defined within the swap and composed operations framework.
- **Governing law**: Australian law, incorporating wholesale lending exemptions and customary lender protections.
- **Operational controls**: Drawdowns require signatures from authorised wallets; multi-sig policies and transaction logs provide auditability.
- **Token lifecycle**: Credit token may be paused or burned following facility repayment or default, aligning ledger state with legal obligations.
