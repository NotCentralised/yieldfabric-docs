# Common Definitions Schedule

Capitalised terms used across the YieldFabric structured receivables legal documents have the following meanings unless the context otherwise requires:

- **Trust** – The YieldFabric Structured Receivables Trust established under the Trust Deed.
- **Trustee** – YieldFabric SPV Pty Ltd (ACN [●]) in its capacity as trustee of the Trust, and its successors or permitted assigns.
- **Manager** – YieldFabric Asset Management Pty Ltd (ACN [●]) or any successor appointed under the Investment Management Agreement to manage the assets of the Trust.
- **Originator** – The entity that sources, originates, and proposes receivables for sale to the Trust pursuant to the Invoice Purchase Agreement.
- **Payer** – The obligor under an invoice or receivable acquired by the Trust.
- **Investor** – A wholesale investor admitted to the Trust pursuant to the Subscription Agreement and holding one or more Investment Tokens.
- **Beneficiary** – A holder of Investment Tokens recorded on the permissioned ledger and entitled to distributions from the Trust.
- **Collateral Agent** – YieldFabric Custody Pty Ltd (ACN [●]) or any successor appointed under the Collateral Security Deed to hold and enforce security on behalf of Investors.
- **Platform Operator** – YieldFabric Technologies Ltd or its permitted delegate that operates and maintains the permissioned ledger and smart-contract infrastructure supporting the Tokens.
- **Issuer Account** – The ledger account or bank account controlled by the Trustee (or Manager under delegated authority) from which issuer-side cash flows are administered.
- **Collateral Account** – The ledger account or bank account controlled by the Collateral Agent holding collateral assets securing obligations to Investors.
- **Investment Token** – A non-fungible token conferring beneficial entitlement to distributions from the issuer account and evidencing the Investor’s interest in the Trust.
- **Credit Token** – A non-fungible token representing rights under the revolving credit facility made available by the Trust and governed by the Credit Deed Poll.
- **Collateral Token** – A non-fungible token granting beneficial rights over collateral assets held within the collateral account and subject to the Collateral Security Deed.
- **Invoice Token** – A non-fungible token embodying the receivable acquired under an Invoice Purchase Agreement, including embedded metadata describing the underlying obligation.
- **Tokens** – Collectively, the Investment Token, Credit Token, Collateral Token, Invoice Token, and any other non-fungible tokens issued under the programme.
- **Credit Facility** – The revolving facility documented in the Credit Deed Poll and accessible via the Credit Token.
- **Receivable** – Any invoice or payment obligation purchased by the Trust from an Originator and evidenced by an Invoice Token.
- **Swap** – A smart-contract mediated transaction exchanging specified obligations, payments, or Tokens between parties under the platform rules.
- **Composed Operation** – An orchestrated sequence of smart-contract actions executed atomically to settle swaps, transfer Tokens, or complete related obligations.
- **Ledger** – The YieldFabric permissioned blockchain on which Tokens, swaps, and related operations are recorded with auditable transaction history.
- **Wallet** – A cryptographic key pair or custodial account authorised to hold Tokens and initiate platform operations.
- **Information Memorandum** – The disclosure document describing the structured receivables investment offer to wholesale investors.
- **Subscription Agreement** – The agreement executed by each Investor to subscribe for Investment Tokens and commit capital to the Trust.
- **Trust Deed** – The deed constituting the Trust and governing the rights and obligations of the Trustee and Beneficiaries.
- **Investment Management Agreement** – The agreement between the Trustee and Manager governing discretionary management of Trust assets.
- **Invoice Purchase Agreement** – The agreement under which the Trust acquires receivables from an Originator.
- **Collateral Security Deed** – The deed granting security over collateral assets to the Collateral Agent for the benefit of Investors.
- **Credit Deed Poll** – The deed poll pursuant to which the Trustee makes the Credit Facility available to holders of Credit Tokens.
- **Swap and Token Operation Terms** – The annex setting out the operational rules for swaps, composed operations, token transfers, identity controls, and smart-contract governance.
- **AML/CTF Programme** – The documented anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing framework maintained by the Trustee and related parties for the programme.
- **Privacy Notice** – The privacy management statement describing how personal information and ledger metadata are collected, used, and protected.

Where a term is defined both in this schedule and in a specific document, the definition in that document prevails to the extent of any inconsistency for the purposes of that document alone.
