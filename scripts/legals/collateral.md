Collateral rights evidenced by the `collateral_token`, anchoring investor protections in the structure. Capitalised terms used in this summary and not otherwise defined have the meanings given in the Common Definitions Schedule.

- **Collateral pool**: Assets maintained within the collateral group, including the factored invoice receivable post-transfer.
- **Beneficial rights**: NFT holder receives all income and recoveries from collateral assets, subject to platform distribution rules.
- **Perfection**: Transfers recorded on YieldFabric and supported by collateral agreements/security deeds granting enforceable interests under Australian law.
- **Priority**: Structured to provide senior claims for investors funding through the investment token, aligning with swap completion steps.
- **Control**: Collateral group membership is restricted to authorised parties; any transfer requires platform approval and compliance checks.
- **Remedies**: Upon obligor default, collateral agent (as defined in accompanying agreements) may enforce receivables, apply proceeds to investor balances, and pursue additional recovery actions.
- **Token governance**: `collateral_token` incorporates policy flags enabling the Collateral Agent to pause, reassign, or burn the NFT in accordance with enforcement outcomes.
- **Data integrity**: Collateral asset schedules linked via hashed references to ensure any amendment or substitution is transparent to token holders.
